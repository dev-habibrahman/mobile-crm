Design Principles

Action First

Users come to perform actions, not read data. Primary actions are always visible and thumb-accessible.

Progressive Disclosure

Show only the most important information first. Reveal secondary details when needed through accordions.

Thumb-Friendly

Designed for one-handed operation. Critical UI anchored to the bottom 30% of the screen.

Fast Scanning

Users can identify any contact in under 2 seconds with clear visual hierarchy and status badges.

Reduce Cognitive Load

Clear spacing, consistent hierarchy, and generous whitespace throughout the experience.
